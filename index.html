What you get

Three things highlight reels never show you.

A goal or an assist tells you what happened. It doesn't tell you why it worked, or what was open that you missed. That's the part we look at.

Positioning

Where you stood relative to the ball, your marker, and the shape of the team — frame by frame, not just on the highlight clips.

Decisions

The pass you played versus the one that was on. Why a choice made sense in the moment, or what made it the wrong read.

Runs

The movement off the ball that never shows up in a box score — the runs that pull defenders, and the ones you left on the table.

How it works

Three clips. One sit-down. No dashboard to learn.

This isn't software you log into. It's footage you send us, and a walkthrough you get back.

STEP — SUBMIT

Send your footage

Upload a clip directly or drop a link — full match, a half, or just the moments you want eyes on. Tell us your position and what you want feedback on.

STEP — REVIEW

We watch it twice

Once for the obvious moments, once for the picture around you — where teammates and defenders were when you made each decision.

STEP — DELIVER

You get the breakdown, your way

A written report, a phone call, or a narrated video with animations over the footage as we talk — pick whichever fits how you actually learn.
